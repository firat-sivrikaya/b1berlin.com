KIDY

KIDY is one of the most skilled DJs in the world, which makes his performance exclusive and memorable. KIDY turns each performance into a full-fledged show. Over his 20-year career, he has evolved from a DJ into a real artist, who feels and sees the audience through and through and knows what needs to be done 1000%.

His songs are played by DJs all over the planet, and they can also be heard on top radio stations and on various charts. He performs at the main clubs of Dubai, hosts his own radio show every Friday in prime time on Pure Ibiza Radio (94.7 FM). He toured in Japan, Thailand, India, Bahrain, Oman, Turkey, and a dozen other countries. He flew all over Russia and the CIS with performances.
