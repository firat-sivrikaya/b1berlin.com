What We Do

Tailored Solutions

Artist Management

Building careers in electronic music.

As a boutique music agency founded in 2022, we offer comprehensive artist management services focused on elevating your presence in the electronic music scene. We handle everything from booking and career planning to contract negotiations and promotional strategies. Our personalized approach means we work closely with each artist to develop tailored management solutions that align with your unique style and career goals. Whether you're an emerging talent looking to break into the scene or an established artist seeking dedicated management, our team provides the expertise, connections, and passion to help you navigate the industry and reach new heights in your musical journey.

Event Organization

Creating memorable electronic music experiences.

B1Berlin specializes in organizing exceptional electronic music events that resonate with audiences and artists alike. From intimate club nights to larger festivals, we handle every aspect of event planning, production, and execution. Our team works diligently to secure the perfect venues, curate compelling artist lineups, manage technical requirements, and implement effective marketing strategies to ensure maximum attendance and impact. With a deep understanding of Berlin's vibrant electronic music scene, we create immersive experiences that foster connections within the community while providing valuable platforms for artists to showcase their talents. Let us transform your event vision into a reality that leaves a lasting impression.

Digital Services

Technology solutions for modern artists.

B1Berlin offers a comprehensive suite of digital services designed specifically for electronic music artists at all career stages. Our offerings include professional website development tailored to your artistic identity, social media management to grow and engage your audience, and content creation services that capture your unique sound and style. We also provide specialized mixing and mastering services to ensure your music reaches its full potential, as well as strategic consultation to help navigate the digital landscape of the music industry. Whether you're looking to establish your online presence for the first time or enhance your existing digital footprint, our team combines technical expertise with industry knowledge to deliver solutions that amplify your artistic vision and connect you with fans worldwide.
