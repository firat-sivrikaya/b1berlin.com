Demayä

Demayä is a ground breaking DJ-Producer who’s aiming at defying genres and pushing all boundaries of sonic possibilities. His fearless approach helped him to quickly gain recognition from the electronic scene. Tracks such as "Desperado" released on Blondish’s Abracadabra, "Lotus" coproduced with his life long friend Moojo or "Now That We Found love" are the embodiment of his afro and latin influences and a perfect blend of organic drums, percussion, ethnic beats and solid Berlin style grooves. He’s traveling the world non stop to share his vison and music. His latest track " You Are" has been praised by his peers, from Black Coffee, Rampa, Awen and Laolu to Diplo, DJ Koze and Pete Tong who have been hammering it since release day. Demayä is on the rise: 250k monthly listeners strong on Spotify only, a remix of Martin Solveig’s Linda and lots of new projects will help him reach new heights.

B1Berlin is proud to represent Demayä in Germany and Turkey.
